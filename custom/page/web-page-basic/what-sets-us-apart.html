City Environment

Our patients are our top priority. We strive to make everyone feel cared for, comforted, and high-spirited.

The moment you walk through our doors, you’re greeted with bright colours, smiling staff, and laughing children. City Orthodontics and Pediatric Dentistry was designed and built to be child friendly and also adult friendly. We have themed rooms, play areas, TVs in every room, and gifts and treats for our young patients. For adults, we have private and semiprivate operatories, and closed and quiet waiting areas.

City Technology

  • 3D Radiographs with 3D face imaging
  • Paperless office (we love trees!)
  • 3D digital impressions of teeth
  • Patient login – patients can always stay connected with us, review their appointments, and submit inquiries through our patient login service.
  • Appointment reminder systems with e-mail and SMS
  • City sMiles is an incentive program that encourages patient education and compliance with oral health. It makes kids, teenagers, and adults involved in their treatment by keeping it fun and engaging. Log in to learn more.
