How to Talk to Your Child about Dentistry

Keep a positive attitude

We understand how intimidating visiting the dentist can be for children. When preparing your child for his or her first dental visit, try not to include too many details. Keep it simple and positive! This ensures your child doesn't experience any undue fear or anxiety prior to his or her visit.
